About Katte

Katte Noel is a non-union voice actor based in Nashville, TN who is currently represented by Wehmann Voice, Helen Wells Agency, and Jackson Agency. Following a childhood packed with stage plays, girls' choir, and musicals, she graduated summa cum laude from the College of Charleston with a B.A. in Theatre Performance.

Since then, Katte used her lifelong skills to train to lend her voice to any medium, including commercials, narration, e-learning, video games, animation, and ADR. She strives to impress every client with her nuanced performance, quick delivery speed, and concise communication, so that whenever they are in need of voiceover, they inevitably return to Katte.

In her free time she likes to play video games, brew coffee, and dabble in game development.

If you find yourself wondering who you should cast for your next project, let Katte provide the answer.
